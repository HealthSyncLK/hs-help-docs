# Provider-Specific FAQ

Common questions from healthcare providers about using HealthSync.

## General Questions

### Is HealthSync suitable for my practice?
HealthSync works for:
- Solo practitioners
- Group practices
- Hospitals and health systems
- Specialty practices
- Any healthcare provider

### How much does it cost for providers?
Provider accounts are free. Patients manage their own subscriptions.

### Is HealthSync HIPAA compliant?
Yes, fully HIPAA compliant with BAA available. See [HIPAA Compliance](../policies-security/hipaa-compliance-overview.md).

## Account Setup

### How long does verification take?
Credential verification typically takes 24-48 hours.

### What credentials do I need?
- Valid medical license
- NPI number
- Practice information
- Professional liability insurance

### Can I have multiple practice locations?
Yes, you can manage multiple locations under one provider account.

## Patient Management

### How do patients connect with me?
- Patients send connection requests
- You can invite patients via email
- Import patient list (with consent)

### Can I see all my patient's records?
Only records patients explicitly share with you.

### What if a patient revokes access?
You lose access immediately. Your existing notes remain in your records.

## Clinical Documentation

### Can I use templates?
Yes, customizable templates available for visit notes.

### Are my notes private?
You control what's shared. Mark sections private or delay sharing.

### Can I attach documents?
Yes, upload supporting documents, images, and reports.

## E-Prescribing

### Is e-prescribing supported?
Yes, integrated with major pharmacy networks.

### Can I prescribe controlled substances?
Yes, with proper DEA certification and verification.

### What about state-specific regulations?
HealthSync complies with all state prescribing regulations.

## Lab Integration

### Which labs are supported?
Major labs including Quest, LabCorp, and most hospital systems.

### How quickly do results appear?
Real-time for integrated labs, or upload manually.

### Can patients see results before me?
You can configure whether results are patient-visible before review.

## Billing and Insurance

### Does HealthSync replace my EHR?
No, it's a supplement. Export notes to your EHR as needed.

### Can I bill through HealthSync?
Not currently. HealthSync focuses on record management and patient engagement.

### Is there Integration with billing systems?
API available for custom integrations, Contact enterprise sales.

## Security and Privacy

### Where is data stored?
Secure US-based data centers with full encryption.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, export all clinical documentation anytime.

### What about malpractice concerns?
HealthSync documentation meets legal medical record standards.

## Technical Questions

### Is there a mobile app for providers?
Yes, iOS and Android apps available.

### Can I use HealthSync on my tablet?
Yes, fully responsive design for all devices.

### What browsers are supported?
All modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

## Support

### What support is available?
- 24/7 email support
- Phone support during business hours
- Dedicated provider success team
- Regular training webinars

### How do I report technical issues?
Email providersupport@healthsync.com or call our provider hotline.

### Is training available?
Yes, free onboarding and training for all providers.
