# Adding Visit Notes

Document patient visits and consultations directly in HealthSync.

## Creating Visit Notes

### Step-by-Step
1. Log in to Provider Portal
2. Select patient from your list
3. Click "New Visit Note"
4. Fill in visit details
5. Save and send to patient

## Visit Note Template

### Required Fields
- Date and time of visit
- Chief complaint
- Diagnosis (ICD-10 codes)
- Treatment plan
- Follow-up instructions

### Optional Fields
- Physical examination findings
- Assessment and plan
- Medications prescribed
- Tests ordered
- Referrals made
- Provider signature

## Note Types

### Quick Notes
Brief documentation for routine visits:
- Follow-up appointments
- Medication refills
- Status checks

### Comprehensive Notes
Detailed documentation for:
- Initial consultations
- Complex diagnoses
- Surgical procedures
- Specialist evaluations

### Templates
Pre-configured templates for:
- Annual physicals
- Sick visits
- Chronic disease management
- Preventive care

## Sharing Visit Notes

### Automatic Sharing
Visit notes automatically shared with patients upon completion.

### Privacy Settings
- Mark sections as private
- Delay sharing until reviewed
- Redact sensitive information
- Add patient-friendly summaries

## Editing and Amendments

### Edit Window
- Notes can be edited within 24 hours
- After 24 hours, use amendment feature
- All changes logged in audit trail

### Addendums
- Add additional information
- Correct errors
- Clarify previous entries
- Maintain original note integrity

## Compliance

:::info
All visit notes comply with medical documentation standards and HIPAA regulations.
:::
