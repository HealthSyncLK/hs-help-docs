# Connecting with Patients

Learn how healthcare providers can connect with patients on HealthSync.

## For Healthcare Providers

HealthSync's DocSync feature allows healthcare providers to:
- Connect securely with patients
- Receive patient records
- Share visit notes and prescriptions
- View patient health timelines

## Registration Process

### Creating a Provider Account
1. Visit HealthSync Provider Portal
2. Complete provider registration
3. Verify credentials (NPI number, medical license)
4. Await approval (24-48 hours)
5. Set up provider profile

### Required Documentation
- Medical license (current and valid)
- NPI (National Provider Identifier)
- Practice information
- Professional liability insurance

## Connecting with Patients

### Patient-Initiated Connection
Patients can invite you to access their records:
1. Patient sends connection request
2. You receive email notification
3. Accept request in provider portal
4. Gain access to authorized records

### Provider-Initiated Connection
You can invite patients:
1. Enter patient's email in provider portal
2. Patient receives invitation
3. Patient accepts and grants permissions
4. Connection established

## Access Permissions

### What You Can See
- Records patient has shared with you
- Visit notes you've created
- Documents tagged for your review
- Patient health timeline

### What You Cannot See
- Records not explicitly shared
- Other providers' notes (unless shared)
- Family member records (without permission)

## Managing Patient Connections

- View all connected patients
- Filter by connection status
- Remove connections
- Request additional access
