# Privacy, Consent & Guardianship

Understand privacy rights and consent requirements for family health management.

## Consent Requirements

### Adult Family Members (18+)
- Explicit consent required for account access
- Can revoke access at any time
- Receive all access notifications
- Control their own sharing permissions

### Minor Children (Under 18)
- Parent/guardian has default access
- No consent required from minor
- Transitions to adult consent at 18
- Special protections for sensitive information

## Guardian Rights

Legal guardians have:
- Full access to dependent's records
- Override capabilities for safety
- Ability to share on behalf of dependent
- Responsibility for data accuracy

## Consent Management

### Obtaining Consent
- Email invitation with clear terms
- Digital consent signature
- Option to decline with explanation
- Renewal requirements for ongoing access

### Revoking Consent
- Can be done at any time
- Takes effect immediately
- Notification to all parties
- Records handling options

## Special Circumstances

### Medical Power of Attorney
Upload legal documentation for:
- Healthcare proxy access
- Emergency decision-making rights
- Temporary guardianship

### Emancipated Minors
Special provisions for:
- Early adult status
- Independent consent
- Guardian access termination
