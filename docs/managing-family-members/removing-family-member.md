# Removing a Family Member

Learn how to safely remove a family member from your account.

## Before Removing

Consider these options:
- Temporarily disable access instead
- Export their records first
- Transfer ownership to another account
- Archive instead of delete

## Removal Process

1. **Navigate to Family Members**
   - Go to family member's profile
   - Click "Settings" or "Manage"

2. **Initiate Removal**
   - Select "Remove Family Member"
   - Review warning message
   - Confirm your decision

3. **Choose Data Handling**
   - Keep records in your account (read-only)
   - Transfer records to their own account
   - Permanently delete all records

## What Happens After Removal

- Access immediately revoked
- Shared links deactivated
- Notifications stopped
- Profile marked as inactive

## Recovery Options

### Grace Period
30-day window to restore removed profiles with all data intact.

### Data Export
Always available before permanent deletion.
