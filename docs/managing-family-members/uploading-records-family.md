# Uploading Records for Family Members

Upload and manage health records on behalf of family members.

## Uploading for Others

1. **Switch to Family Member Profile**
   - Use profile selector
   - Select the family member

2. **Upload Documents**
   - Follow standard upload process
   - Documents automatically tagged to selected profile

3. **Review and Categorize**
   - Assign appropriate categories
   - Add relevant tags
   - Set privacy settings

## Bulk Family Uploads

Upload multiple records for different family members:
- Tag documents during upload
- Assign to appropriate profiles
- Batch categorization

## Permission Requirements

Ensure you have proper permissions:
- Full access required for uploads
- Guardian access for minors
- Consent for adult family members

## Organizing Family Records

Best practices:
- Use consistent naming conventions
- Maintain separate categories per person
- Regular updates and reviews
- Backup important documents
