# Plan Comparison & Features

Compare HealthSync subscription plans and choose the right one for you.

## Available Plans

### Free Plan
**$0/month**

Features:
- ✅ Up to 100 documents
- ✅ Basic organization tools
- ✅ 5 AI analyses per month
- ✅ Mobile app access
- ✅ Email support
- ❌ Family member profiles (1 only)
- ❌ Advanced features

### Plus Plan
**$9.99/month**

Features:
- ✅ Unlimited documents
- ✅ Advanced organization
- ✅ 50 AI analyses per month
- ✅ Up to 5 family members
- ✅ Priority email support
- ✅ Document sharing
- ✅ Export capabilities

### Premium Plan
**$19.99/month**

Features:
- ✅ Everything in Plus
- ✅ Unlimited AI analyses
- ✅ Unlimited family members
- ✅ Priority support (24/7)
- ✅ Advanced analytics
- ✅ API access
- ✅ Healthcare provider portal
- ✅ Extended storage

### Enterprise Plan
**Custom pricing**

Features:
- ✅ Everything in Premium
- ✅ Custom integrations
- ✅ Dedicated account manager
- ✅ SLA guarantees
- ✅ White-label options
- ✅ Compliance support
- ✅ Training & onboarding
