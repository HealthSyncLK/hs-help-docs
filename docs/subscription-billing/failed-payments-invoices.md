# Failed Payments & Invoices

Handle payment failures and access your invoice history.

## Common Payment Failures

### Why Payments Fail
- Insufficient funds
- Expired credit card
- Bank declined transaction
- Billing address mismatch
- Card security issues

## When Payment Fails

### What Happens
1. Email notification sent immediately
2. Retry attempted after 3 days
3. Second retry after 7 days
4. Account downgraded if payment still fails

### Grace Period
- 14 days to update payment method
- Full account access maintained
- No data loss during grace period

## Resolving Failed Payments

1. Check email for failure notification
2. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
3. Update payment method
4. Click **Retry Payment**
5. Confirm transaction

## Invoice Management

### Accessing Invoices
- Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing** > **Invoices**
- View all historical invoices
- Download PDF copies
- Email to external addresses

### Invoice Details
Each invoice includes:
- Invoice number
- Billing period
- Itemized charges
- Payment method
- Transaction date
- Download/print options

## Requesting Invoice Changes

Need to update invoice details?
1. Contact support within 7 days
2. Provide correct information
3. Amended invoice issued within 48 hours

## Account Reinstatement

If account was downgraded:
1. Update payment method
2. Pay outstanding balance
3. Resubscribe to desired plan
4. All data restored immediately
