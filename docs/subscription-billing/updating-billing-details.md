# Updating Billing Details

Manage your payment methods and billing information.

## Payment Methods

### Supported Options
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (Enterprise only)

## Updating Payment Information

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Payment Methods**
3. Select **Add New Card** or **Edit**
4. Enter card details securely
5. Set as default payment method
6. Save changes

## Billing Address

Update your billing address:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Edit Billing Address**
3. Enter new address
4. Save changes

## Viewing Invoices

Access your payment history:
- All past invoices available in billing section
- Download PDFs for your records
- Email invoices to accounting department

## Auto-Renewal

Manage subscription renewal:
- Toggle auto-renewal on/off
- Choose billing frequency
- Update renewal date
- Receive renewal reminders

## Security

:::info
All payment information is encrypted and processed securely through Stripe. HealthSync does not store complete card numbers.
:::
