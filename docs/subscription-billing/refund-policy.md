# Refund Policy

Understand HealthSync's refund and cancellation policy.

## Refund Eligibility

### 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee
Full refund available if:
- First-time subscriber
- Within 30 days of initial purchase
- Premium or Plus plan
- No prior refund history

### Pro-Rated Refunds
Available for:
- Annual subscriptions canceled within 90 days
- Downgrade from Premium to Plus
- Calculation based on unused months

### No Refunds
- Free plan (no charges)
- After 30-day window for monthly plans
- Enterprise custom agreements (see contract)
- Add-on purchases

## Requesting a Refund

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Select reason for cancellation
4. Check "Request refund"
5. Submit request

### Processing Time
- Review within 2 business days
- Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
- Refund appears on original payment method

## Cancellation Options

### Cancel Anytime
- No cancellation fees
- Access until end of billing period
- Auto-renewal stops
- Data retained for 90 days

### Temporary Pause
- Pause subscription up to 3 months
- Keep your data
- Resume anytime
- Available for Plus and Premium

## What Happens After Cancellation

### Immediate
- Auto-renewal disabled
- No future charges

### End of Billing Period
- Account downgraded to Free plan
- Premium features disabled
- Data retained (within Free plan limits)

### After 90 Days
- Extended data archived
- Can be restored within 1 year
- After 1 year, permanent deletion

## Reactivation

Easy to return:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Subscription**
3. Choose new plan
4. All data restored immediately
