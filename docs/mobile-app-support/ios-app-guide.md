# iOS App Guide

Complete guide to using HealthSync on iPhone and iPad.

## Download and Installation

### Requirements
- iOS 14.0 or later
- iPadOS 14.0 or later
- 50MB free storage space
- Internet connection

### Installing the App
1. Open App Store
2. Search for "HealthSync"
3. Tap "Get" then "Install"
4. Authenticate with Face ID/Touch ID
5. Open app when installation completes
6. Sign in with your account

## Getting Started

### First Launch
1. Sign in or create account
2. Grant necessary permissions:
   - Photos (for document uploads)
   - Camera (for scanning)
   - Health (for Apple Health integration)
   - Notifications (for alerts)
3. Complete quick tour
4. Optional: Enable Apple Health sync

## Key Features

### Document Scanning
- Tap camera icon
- Built-in iOS document scanner
- Auto-detect edges
- Perspective correction
- Multi-page scanning
- Sign and markup documents

### Quick Actions
3D Touch/Haptic Touch on app icon:
- Upload Document
- Scan Document
- View Recent
- AI Analysis

### Spotlight Search
Search documents directly from iOS Spotlight.

### Siri Shortcuts
Create custom Siri shortcuts:
- "Upload my lab results"
- "Show recent records"
- "Run AI analysis"

### Widgets
Add widgets to home screen:
- Recent Documents widget (Small, Medium, Large)
- AI Analysis widget
- Quick Upload widget

## Navigation

### Tab Bar
- **Home**: Dashboard
- **Records**: All documents
- **Family**: Family profiles
- **AI**: Analysis results
- **More**: Settings

### Gestures
- Swipe right: Go back
- Pull down: Refresh
- Long press: Quick actions
- Swipe left on record: Share/Delete

## iOS-Specific Features

### Apple Health Integration
Sync health data with Apple Health:
- Lab results
- Vital signs
- Medical records
- Immunizations

Enable in: Settings > Integrations > Apple Health

### iCloud Sync
- Automatic backup to iCloud
- Sync between iPhone and iPad
- Restore data on new device

### Face ID / Touch ID
Secure app access with biometrics:
- Enable in Settings > Security
- Require on launch
- Require for sensitive actions

### Share Sheet
Share documents via iOS Share Sheet:
- AirDrop
- Messages
- Email
- Other apps

### Handoff
Start on iPhone, continue on iPad (and vice versa).

## iPad-Specific Features

### Split View / Slide Over
- Use HealthSync alongside other apps
- Drag and drop documents
- Multi-tasking support

### Apple Pencil
- Annotate documents
- Sign forms
- Markup reports
- Take notes

### Keyboard Shortcuts
Connect keyboard for shortcuts:
- Cmd+N: New upload
- Cmd+F: Search
- Cmd+,: Settings

## Notifications

### Notification Types
- Upload complete
- AI analysis ready
- Share access
- Family member activity
- Document expiration reminders

### Manage Notifications
Settings > Notifications > Group by category

## Troubleshooting

### App Not Opening
- Force close and reopen
- Restart device
- Check for updates
- Reinstall if necessary

### Sync Issues
- Check internet connection
- Verify iCloud sync is enabled
- Sign out and sign back in
- Contact support

### Camera Issues
- Grant camera permission in Settings
- Clean camera lens
- Ensure adequate lighting
- Use manual photo mode

## Performance Tips

- Enable Low Power Mode support
- Download documents for offline access
- Use Wi-Fi for large uploads
- Clear cache regularly

## Accessibility

Full iOS accessibility support:
- VoiceOver
- Dynamic Type
- Reduce Motion
- High Contrast
- Switch Control
