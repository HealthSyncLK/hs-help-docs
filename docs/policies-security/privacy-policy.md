# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 27, 2026**

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how HealthSync collects, uses, and protects your personal health information.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Name and email address
- Phone number (optional)
- Payment information
- Account preferences

### Health Information
- Medical documents you upload
- Health records and reports
- AI analysis results
- Family member health data

### Usage Information
- App usage analytics
- Device information
- IP addresses
- Browser type and version

### Third-Party Data
- Healthcare provider records (with consent)
- Lab results (from integrated services)
- Wearable device data (if connected)

## How We Use Your Information

### Primary Uses
- Provide HealthSync services
- AI analysis of medical documents
- Sync across your devices
- Customer support
- Account management

### Secondary Uses
- Improve our services
- Develop new features
- Ensure security
- Comply with legal obligations
- Communicate updates

### Never Used For
- ❌ Selling your data to third parties
- ❌ Advertising purposes
- ❌ Sharing with insurance companies
- ❌ Employment screening
- ❌ Non-authorized marketing

## Data Sharing

### When We Share Your Data

**With Your Consent**
- Healthcare providers you authorize
- Family members you grant access
- Third parties you choose to share with

**Service Providers**
- Cloud hosting (AWS, encrypted)
- Payment processing (Stripe)
- Email services (SendGrid)
- Analytics (anonymized only)

**Legal Requirements**
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Legal compliance
- Protect rights and safety
- Fraud prevention

### When We Don't Share
- Never sell your data
- Never share for marketing
- Never provide to insurers without consent
- Never share with employers

## Your Privacy Rights

### Access Your Data
- Download all your data anytime
- View data sharing history
- See access logs

### Control Your Data
- Edit or delete information
- Revoke sharing permissions
- Close your account
- Opt out of communications

### Data Portability
- Export in standard formats (PDF, JSON, CSV)
- Transfer to other services
- No lock-in

### Right to Be Forgotten
- Request complete data deletion
- 30-day grace period
- Permanent removal after 90 days
- Legal retention exceptions apply

## Children's Privacy

- HealthSync is for users 18+
- Parental/guardian consent required for minors
- Special protections for children's data
- Compliance with COPPA

## Cookies and Tracking

### Essential Cookies
- Authentication
- Security
- Session management
- Preferences

### Optional Cookies
- Analytics (can opt out)
- Performance monitoring
- User experience improvements

Manage: Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences

## Data Security

See [Data Protection & Encryption](./data-protection-encryption.md) for details.

## International Users

### Data Transfers
- Data stored in US data centers
- Transfers comply with GDPR
- Standard contractual clauses
- Privacy Shield framework

### Regional Rights
- GDPR (EU residents)
- CCPA (California residents)
- Other applicable privacy laws

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy:
- Email notification of changes
- 30-day notice for material changes
- Continued use implies acceptance
- Can close account if disagree
