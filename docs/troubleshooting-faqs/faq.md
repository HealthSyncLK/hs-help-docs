# Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about HealthSync.

## General Questions

### What is HealthSync?
HealthSync is a secure platform for storing, organizing, and understanding your medical records using AI technology.

### Is HealthSync HIPAA compliant?
Yes, HealthSync is fully HIPAA compliant. Learn more in our [HIPAA Compliance Overview](../policies-security/hipaa-compliance-overview.md).

### How much does HealthSync cost?
We offer a free plan with basic features and paid plans starting at $9.99/month. See [Plan Comparison](../subscription-billing/plan-comparison-features.md).

### Is my data secure?
Yes, all data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption. See [Data Protection](../policies-security/data-protection-encryption.md).

## Account Questions

### Can I have multiple accounts?
Each person should have one account. Use family member profiles to manage multiple people's records.

### How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. Note that this action is permanent after 30 days.

### Can I export my data?
Yes, you can export all your data at any time from Settings > Data Export.

## Document Questions

### What file types can I upload?
PDF, JPG, PNG, HEIC, DICOM, HL7, and more. See [Supported Formats](../ai-report-analyzer/supported-document-formats.md).

### How many documents can I store?
Free plan: 100 documents
Plus plan: Unlimited
Premium plan: Unlimited

### Can I scan documents with my phone?
Yes, the mobile app has built-in document scanning. See [Mobile App Guide](../mobile-app-support/intro.md).

## AI Analysis Questions

### How accurate is the AI?
Our AI is highly accurate for standard medical reports but should not replace professional medical advice.

### Does AI work on handwritten notes?
Limited support. Best results with typed or printed text.

### How many AI analyses can I run?
Free: 5/month
Plus: 50/month
Premium: Unlimited

## Sharing Questions

### Can I share with my doctor?
Yes, you can securely share records with healthcare providers. See [Sharing Records](../managing-records/sharing-health-records.md).

### How long do share links last?
You can set expiration dates or create permanent links.

### Can I revoke access?
Yes, you can revoke access to shared records at any time.

## Family Management Questions

### How many family members can I add?
Free: Your own profile only
Plus: Up to 5 family members
Premium: Unlimited

### Do family members need their own accounts?
No, you can manage family member profiles within your account.

### Can family members access my records?
Only if you explicitly grant them permission.

## Technical Questions

### Which browsers are supported?
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions).

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes, available for both iOS and Android.

### Can I use HealthSync offline?
Limited functionality available offline. See [Offline Access](../mobile-app-support/offline-access.md).

## Billing Questions

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees.

### Do you offer refunds?
Yes, 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. See [Refund Policy](../subscription-billing/refund-policy.md).

### What payment methods do you accept?
Credit cards, debit cards, and PayPal.
