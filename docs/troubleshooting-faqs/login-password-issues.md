# Login & Password Issues

Resolve account access and password problems.

## Can't Log In

### Forgot Password
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check inbox for reset link
4. Click link and create new password
5. Log in with new password

### Email Not Recognized
**Possible reasons:**
- Account registered with different email
- Typo in email address
- Account not yet created

**Solution:** Try alternate email addresses or create new account

### Password Reset Email Not Received
- Check spam/junk folder
- Verify correct email address
- Wait 10 minutes and retry
- Contact support if still not received

## Account Locked

### Too Many Failed Attempts
- Account temporarily locked for 15 minutes
- Use "Forgot Password" to reset
- Contact support for immediate unlock

### Suspicious Activity Detected
- Security measure to protect your account
- Verify your identity via email
- Contact support for assistance

## Two-Factor Authentication Issues

### Can't Access 2FA Code
- Use backup codes saved during setup
- Click "Didn't receive code?" for alternatives
- Contact support to disable 2FA

### Lost Device with 2FA App
- Use backup codes
- Verify identity via email
- Support can reset 2FA after verification

## Password Requirements

Your password must:
- Be at least 8 characters long
- Include uppercase and lowercase letters
- Contain at least one number
- Include at least one special character
- Not match common passwords

## Changing Your Password

1. Log in to your account
2. Go to **Settings** > **Security**
3. Click **Change Password**
4. Enter current password
5. Enter and confirm new password
6. Save changes

## Session Expired

If logged out unexpectedly:
- Sessions expire after 30 days of inactivity
- Multiple device logins may conflict
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Log in again
