# Deleting & Updating Records

Learn how to safely delete or update your health records.

## Updating Records

### Editing Metadata
Update record information without changing the original document.

### Replacing Documents
Replace the document file while keeping the record history and metadata.

### Adding Notes
Add personal notes and annotations to any record.

## Deleting Records

### Soft Delete
Records are moved to trash and can be recovered within 30 days.

### Permanent Delete
Permanently remove records from your account after confirmation.

### Bulk Operations
Delete multiple records at once using batch selection.

## Recovery Options

### Restore from Trash
Recover accidentally deleted records within 30 days.

### Version History
View previous versions of edited records (Premium feature).

## Important Notes

:::warning
Permanently deleted records cannot be recovered. Always review carefully before confirming deletion.
:::
