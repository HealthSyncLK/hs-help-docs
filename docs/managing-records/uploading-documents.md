# Uploading Documents

Learn how to upload health records and medical documents to HealthSync.

## Supported File Types

- PDF documents
- Images (JPG, PNG, HEIC)
- DICOM medical imaging files
- HL7 and FHIR standard formats

## Upload Methods

### Drag and Drop
Simply drag files from your computer into the upload area.

### File Browser
Click "Choose Files" to select documents from your device.

### Mobile Upload
Use your phone camera to capture and upload documents directly.

### Email Upload
Forward documents to your unique HealthSync email address.

## Bulk Upload

Upload multiple documents simultaneously for faster organization.

## Document Categorization

Assign categories during upload:
- Lab Results
- Prescriptions
- Imaging Reports
- Vaccination Records
- Visit Notes
