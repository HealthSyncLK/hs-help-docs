# Sharing Health Records

Securely share your health records with healthcare providers and family members.

## Sharing Methods

### Secure Link
Generate a time-limited secure link to specific records.

### Email Share
Send records directly via encrypted email.

### Healthcare Provider Portal
Grant direct access to registered healthcare providers.

### QR Code
Generate a QR code for quick in-person sharing.

## Share Settings

Configure sharing options:
- Expiration date/time
- View-only or download permissions
- Require password protection
- Access notifications

## Managing Active Shares

View and manage all active shares:
- See who has access
- Revoke access anytime
- View access logs
- Extend or modify permissions

## Access Tracking

Monitor who accessed your records:
- View timestamps
- See IP addresses
- Track download activity
- Receive access notifications
