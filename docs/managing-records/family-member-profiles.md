# Family Member Profiles

Manage health records for your entire family in one place.

## Creating Family Profiles

Add family members to your account:
1. Navigate to Family Members section
2. Click "Add Family Member"
3. Enter their information
4. Set permissions and access levels

## Profile Information

Each family member profile includes:
- Personal information
- Health insurance details
- Emergency contacts
- Medical history overview

## Switching Between Profiles

Easily switch between family member profiles using the profile selector.

## Record Organization

Each family member has their own:
- Separate record repository
- Individual AI analysis history
- Custom categories and tags
- Privacy settings
