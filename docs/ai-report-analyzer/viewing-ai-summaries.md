# Viewing AI Summaries

Access and understand your AI-generated health report summaries.

## Accessing Summaries

### From Record View
- Open any analyzed document
- Click "View AI Summary" tab
- Summary appears alongside original document

### From Dashboard
- "Recent AI Analyses" widget
- Shows latest summaries
- Quick access to full reports

## Summary Components

### Key Findings
Highlighted important results and observations.

### Abnormal Values
Flags for test results outside normal ranges with reference values.

### Plain Language Explanation
Medical jargon translated into understandable terms.

### Recommended Actions
Suggested next steps or follow-ups mentioned in the report.

### Timeline
Comparison with previous results to show trends.

## Understanding the Summary

Each summary includes:
- **Confidence Score** - AI's confidence in the analysis
- **Key Terms Glossary** - Definitions of medical terms
- **Visual Indicators** - Color-coded alerts for abnormalities
- **Export Options** - Save or share the summary

## Providing Feedback

Help improve AI accuracy:
- Rate the summary quality
- Report incorrect interpretations
- Suggest improvements
