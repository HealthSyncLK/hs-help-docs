# Best Practices for Interpretation

Get the most accurate and useful AI analysis results.

## Document Preparation

### Quality Matters
- Use high-resolution scans (300 DPI or higher)
- Ensure good contrast and lighting
- Keep text horizontal and legible
- Remove shadows and glare

### Complete Documents
- Include all pages
- Don't crop important information
- Maintain header and footer information
- Keep reference ranges visible

## Understanding AI Limitations

### AI is a Tool, Not a Doctor
- Always consult healthcare professionals
- Don't make medical decisions based solely on AI
- Use summaries as a starting point for discussions
- Verify critical information

### Context Matters
- AI may not understand personal medical history
- Summaries are generalized
- Individual circumstances vary
- Always consider the full picture

## Maximizing Accuracy

### Provide Context
- Tag documents with correct categories
- Add dates of service
- Include provider information
- Note special circumstances

### Review and Verify
- Cross-check AI findings with original document
- Look for obvious errors
- Validate numerical values
- Confirm medical terminology

## Using AI Summaries Effectively

### For Personal Understanding
- Read summaries to understand your health
- Track trends over time
- Identify questions for your doctor
- Prepare for appointments

### For Sharing
- Share both AI summary and original document
- Explain AI summary limitations
- Encourage professional review
- Use as conversation starter
