# How AI Analysis Works

Understand how HealthSync's AI technology analyzes your medical documents.

## AI Technology Overview

HealthSync uses advanced natural language processing and machine learning to:
- Extract key information from medical documents
- Identify important findings and abnormalities
- Generate plain language summaries
- Highlight areas requiring attention

## Analysis Process

1. **Document Upload** - Upload your medical report
2. **Text Extraction** - AI reads and extracts text from the document
3. **Medical Entity Recognition** - Identifies medical terms, values, and conditions
4. **Context Analysis** - Understands relationships between findings
5. **Summary Generation** - Creates easy-to-understand summary

## What AI Can Detect

- Lab test results and reference ranges
- Abnormal findings
- Diagnoses and conditions
- Medication names and dosages
- Recommendations and follow-ups
- Trends over time

## Accuracy and Limitations

:::info
AI analysis is a tool to help you understand your records. Always consult with healthcare professionals for medical advice.
:::
