# Beta Features

Get early access to new features by joining our beta program.

## Currently in Beta

### AI Health Insights (Open Beta)
**Status**: Available to all Premium users

**What it does:**
- Analyzes health trends over time
- Identifies patterns in lab results
- Provides personalized health recommendations
- Predicts potential health concerns

**How to enable:**
Settings > Beta Features > AI Health Insights

**Known issues:**
- May show false positives for rare conditions
- Limited to English language documents
- Requires minimum 3 months of data

**Feedback:** [Report Issue](mailto:beta@healthsync.com?subject=AI%20Health%20Insights)

---

### Voice Commands (Closed Beta)
**Status**: Limited testing (waitlist available)

**What it does:**
- Upload documents via voice
- Search using natural language
- Ask questions about your health records
- Hands-free navigation

**Current capabilities:**
- "Upload my latest lab results"
- "When was my last blood test?"
- "Show me records from Dr. Smith"
- "Run AI analysis on my recent MRI"

**Join waitlist:** Settings > Beta Features > Voice Commands

---

### Family Health Dashboard 2.0 (Open Beta)
**Status**: Available to Plus and Premium users

**New features:**
- Side-by-side health comparisons
- Shared health goals tracking
- Family health timeline
- Genetic health insights
- Medication interaction checker

**How to enable:**
Settings > Beta Features > Family Dashboard 2.0

**Note:** Replaces current family dashboard when enabled

---

### Apple Watch App (Closed Beta)
**Status**: iOS users only, limited spots

**Features:**
- Quick document upload from watch
- Health metric sync
- Medication reminders
- Appointment notifications
- Emergency access to critical records

**Requirements:**
- Apple Watch Series 4 or newer
- watchOS 9.0+
- HealthSync iOS app installed

**Join beta:** TestFlight link via email when accepted

---

### Blockchain Verification (Private Beta)

**Target users:**
- Enterprise customers

**Request access:** enterprise@healthsync.com

## Joining Beta Programs

### Open Beta
- Available to eligible users
- Enable in Settings > Beta Features
- No approval needed
- Can opt out anytime

### Closed Beta
- Limited spots available
- Application or waitlist required
- Selection based on:
  - Account history
  - Usage patterns
  - Compatibility requirements

### Private Beta
- Invitation only
- Usually for enterprise features
- NDA may be required
- Direct contact with HealthSync team

## Beta Guidelines

### What to Expect
- Features may be incomplete
- Bugs and crashes possible
- UI subject to change
- Features may be removed
- Data loss rare but possible

### Best Practices
1. **Don't rely on beta features for critical tasks**
2. **Report bugs promptly**
3. **Provide constructive feedback**
4. **Respect NDAs** (if applicable)
5. **Be patient** - this is work in progress

### Opting Out
- Can leave beta anytime
- Settings > Beta Features > Disable
- Data preserved
- Switch back to stable features

## FAQ

**Q: Are beta features free?**
A: Some require Premium subscription, but beta access is never an additional charge.

**Q: What if a beta feature breaks?**
A: We provide rollback options and support. Critical issues fixed within 24 hours.

**Q: Can I use beta on production data?**
A: Yes, but we recommend caution with mission-critical workflows.

**Q: How do I report bugs?**
A: In-app feedback tool or beta@healthsync.com with details and screenshots.

**Q: Will beta features always be released?**
A: Most graduate to full release, but some may be discontinued based on feedback.
