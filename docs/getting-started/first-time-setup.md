# First-Time Setup

Get started with HealthSync by completing your initial setup.

## Setup Steps

1. **Complete Your Profile** - Add personal information
2. **Upload Your First Record** - Start building your health history
3. **Set Privacy Preferences** - Control who can see your information
4. **Connect Family Members** - Add family profiles if needed
5. **Enable Notifications** - Stay informed about important updates

## Profile Information

Complete your profile with:
- Full name and date of birth
- Contact information
- Emergency contacts
- Insurance details (optional)

## Privacy Settings

Configure your privacy preferences during setup or later in settings.
