# Account Creation & Login

Learn how to create your HealthSync account and log in securely.

![Account Creation and Login](/img/login/test1.png)

## Creating a New Account

1. Visit the HealthSync website or open the mobile app
2. Click on "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
3. Enter your email address and create a strong password
4. Verify your email address through the verification link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile information

## Logging In

Enter your registered email and password to access your account.

### Forgot Password?

Click on "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions to reset it.

## Two-Factor Authentication

Enable 2FA for enhanced security in your account settings.
